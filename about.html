About KoKoro Collective CIC


Kokoro Collective is focused on meeting the needs and demands of the people within the ruralities of Herefordshire and Worcestershire.
Kokoro is a Japanese word which translates into English as; ’Mind, Body and Soul’.
This is essentially the service that the practitioners of Kokoro provide which offer a broad array of well-being treatments. This comes under the banner of “Social Prescribing.”
Our aim is to offer well-being events for all of the community, supporting the loneliness and isolation projects currently running for people suffering with chronic illness or disabilities, physically or mentally. Once we are free from Lockdown we will be holding events at Village Halls, Community Halls and Churches with aspirations towards signposting and working alongside other agencies within the area.
